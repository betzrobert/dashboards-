Key Findings — Combined View

  • Major week-over-week improvement: Day 1 attendance jumped from 81.4% (4.27) to 98.1% (5.11) — a +16.7 point gain. Days 2 and 3 also improved markedly (Day 3: 74.6% → 91.7%).
  • Cohort size shrank meaningfully: 4.27 had 118 expected attendees across 8 locations; 5.11 had 52 across 6 locations. Smaller cohorts are easier to manage, but the rate improvement extends beyond pure size effect.
  • CoAssist Pharmacy improved modestly but is now concentrated: Group Day 1 rate held at 83.6% → 86.4%. In 4.27 it spanned CoAssist + Remote Hub + Remote Non-Hub (55 expected); in 5.11 it again spans the same 3 locations but at 22 expected. CoAssist (the location) is still the weakest performer (64% then 87%).
  • PS-Academy: dramatic improvement in a smaller cohort: From 38/47 (80.9%) on 4.27 to 10/9 (111.1%) on 5.11. The 5.11 cohort was Maitland-only (no SouthPark Advance Academy track), and a contractor showed up unexpectedly. Day 2/3 normalized to 100%.
  • Late adds appeared on 5.11: Maitland (+1 contractor not on EFC) and SouthPark (+1, Katherine Cruz late add). Net Day 1 variance was zero (2 CoAssist no-shows offset by 2 unexpected adds). Worth tracking whether late adds become a pattern.
  • Inactive groups across both weeks: ARx PS Pharmacy had no cohort either week. Commercial/Support/Tech had a 3-person Arizona cohort on 4.27 (100% attendance) but none on 5.11. SouthPark Advance (the 4-week PS-Academy track) had no cohort on 5.11.
  • Mapping assumptions to confirm: SouthPark Advance treated as PS-Academy at SPO. DSM in matrix had no cohort either week. ARx PS Pharmacy assignment to OPK not yet validated against actual data. Please confirm or correct.
