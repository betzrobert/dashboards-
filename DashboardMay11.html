Key Findings

  • Strong week overall: Day 1 attendance was 51/52 (98.1%) — a major improvement from the 4.27 cohort (81.4%). Day 2 and Day 3 attendance of those scheduled held above 91% throughout.
  • Maitland (PS-Academy): 9 expected, 10 attended Day 1 — a contractor showed up unexpectedly (not on the EFC). That contractor was non-hub and was not expected for Days 2 or 3, so the count returns to 9 for the rest of the week (100% of scheduled).
  • SouthPark (All Other Pat Sol): 20 expected, 21 attended Day 1 and Day 2 — Katherine Cruz joined late afternoon 5/11 (not on EFC). SPO onsite new hires joined the remote session this week.
  • CoAssist (CoAssist Pharmacy): 15 expected, 13 attended each day (86.7%) — 2 no-shows. Onsite facilitation occurred. This is the only group/location with a notable Day 1 gap.
  • Net Day 1 variance is zero: 2 CoAssist no-shows were offset by 2 unexpected late adds (Maitland contractor + SouthPark Katherine Cruz). Headline 51/52 reflects this net.
  • Smaller cohort, different mix: Only 52 expected this week (vs. 118 on 4.27) and 3 of 5 training groups active. No Commercial/Support/Tech cohort (Arizona had no expected attendees); no ARx PS Pharmacy cohort. CoAssist Pharmacy and All Other Pat Sol dominated the week.
