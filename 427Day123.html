Key Findings

  • Arizona (PHX, Commercial/Support/Tech): Only site with 100% attendance across all 3 days — perfect benchmark for the simplest training pathway (Day 1 HR only).
  • PS-Academy cohort (47 expected, all 3 days scheduled): Largest group; attendance drifted from 38 → 39 → 37 across the week (80.9% → 83.0% → 78.7%). Notable: Day 2 attendance at SouthPark Advance (32) actually exceeded Day 1 (31), suggesting a Day 1 late-add or a re-engaged no-show.
  • CoAssist Pharmacy cohort (55 expected): Day 1 attendance 46/55 (83.6%). Only CoAssist (the location, 11 expected) was scheduled for Day 3 — they hit 7/11 (63.6%), the lowest scheduled Day 3 rate. Remote Hub and Remote Non-Hub (44 of the 55) were not required after Day 2.
  • All Other Pat Sol cohort (13 expected): Lowest Day 1 group rate at 9/13 (69.2%), driven by SouthPark (7/10) and Overland Park (2/3). Of these, only SouthPark was scheduled for Day 3 (6/10 = 60%).
  • Overall daily trend: Day 1 = 96/118 (81.4%), Day 2 = 95/116 (81.9%, Remote Non-Hub excluded), Day 3 = 53/71 (74.6%, three locations excluded). Attendance of those required held remarkably stable from Day 1 to Day 2; the Day 3 dip is concentrated at CoAssist and SouthPark.
  • Mapping assumptions to confirm: SouthPark Advance treated as PS-Academy at SPO (based on the 4-week Academy program naming). DSM in matrix had no cohort this week. ARx PS Pharmacy (matrix shows OPK) had no attendees in this dataset — please confirm or correct the assignments.
