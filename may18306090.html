30-Day Trends
▲ 30-Day Overall surged to 92.0% in Wave 4 — new all-time high (+8.1 pts vs Wave 1)

After dipping to 78.8% in Wave 2, the 30-day experience fully recovered and now exceeds any prior result. "Made the right decision" hit 94%, "Would recommend" 92%.

▲ 30-Day Onboarding Experience: 84.4% → 81.7% → 81.1% → 90.3%

After two refreshes of decline, a 9.2-point surge in Wave 4. "Accurate info during recruitment" jumped from 66% to 82%.

▲ 30-Day Team & Culture: 85.7% → 80.6% → 81.6% → 91.3% — New high

"Team members and leaders supportive" hit 94% in Wave 4. All three sub-items exceed 88%.

▲ Training item recovering: 65% → 59% → 67% → 76%

"Efficient training on procedures" rose 17 points from its Wave 2 low.

60-Day Trends
▼ 60-Day Onboarding Experience: 78.9% → 75.6% → 75.2% → 71.7% — Declined every refresh

"Role expectations communicated through onboarding" stuck at 63–66% across all waves. Has not responded to any intervention so far.

▼ 60-Day Overall: 82.5% → 83.2% → 81.9% → 78.1% — Significant drop in Wave 4

"I feel I belong at AssistRx" dropped to 73.8% — the lowest belonging score in the full dataset. Extreme driver correlation with Overall.

▼ 60-Day Team & Culture: 88.3% → 87.8% → 87.2% → 83.3% — Slow consistent erosion

5 points lower than Wave 1. "Felt welcomed since starting" dropped from 90% to 83%.

● "Discussed development goals" flat: 52% → 48% → 51% → 52.8% at 60-Day

Has never exceeded 53% in any 60-day wave across 6 months of data. Most stubborn item in the dataset.
