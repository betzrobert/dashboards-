Strengths
▲ Team & Culture is the standout: 95.2% — +14.1 pts over the company

Every culture sub-item scores at or near 100%. "My team members and leaders have been supportive" and "I feel included at AssistRx" both hit 100%.

▲ Communication leads the company by +9.2 pts (90.5% vs 81.3%)

"Communication is clear from direct leadership" and "Direct leadership is easily accessible" both at 100%. Tech managers are visibly present.

▲ Relationships and environment are perfect scores

"Built strong relationships with my team," "appreciate the work environment," and "comfortable asking questions" all at 100% — well above company.

Watch Areas
▼ Development conversations are the #1 gap: 42.9% — −16.8 pts vs company

Only 3 of 7 tech hires discussed development goals with their leader. This is the lowest single question in the tech dataset and a Very Strong driver of Overall.

▼ Onboarding readiness lags: resources sufficient & "prepared me for my role" both at 57.1%

Roughly 4 of 7 felt set up. These pull Onboarding Experience down to 81.6% despite perfect relationship scores.

● Professional Development is the only factor below company (77.1% vs 77.2%)

Career-growth belief is solid, but "opportunities for advancement" (71.4%) and the development-goals conversation drag the factor.

● Top checklist gaps: tools/software, building access & assigned help (each 50%)

Half of tech hires flagged at least one logistics gap. "Introduced to my team" was the one item nobody flagged (0%).
